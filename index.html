Privacy Policy for instdict

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

At instdict, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects any data while delivering instant word definitions.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data: instdict does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.
  • Ephemeral Data Access: The extension utilizes the activeTab permission only to access the content of the current page when you explicitly request a definition, and the contextMenus permission to add a “Look Up Definition” option. These permissions are used solely for on-demand functionality and do not result in any data being permanently stored.

2. How We Use the Information

  • ActiveTab Permission: Enables instdict to temporarily read the active page content to capture the selected word for lookup purposes.
  • ContextMenus Permission: Provides easy access via a right-click menu, allowing you to quickly fetch definitions without impacting your normal browsing activity.

3. Data Security

  • Since instdict only accesses data on-demand and does not retain any information, your privacy and security are maintained.
  • We implement standard security practices to ensure that any temporary data accessed during lookups is handled safely and cleared immediately afterward.

4. Third-Party Services

  • instdict does not share your data with any third-party services. All word lookup processing is performed locally, and no external data is transmitted.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version, so we encourage you to review it periodically.
